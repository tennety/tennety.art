## Description

"Quietly devastating." -- [Optical Sloth](https://opticalsloth.com/?p=28982)

_Tongue-tied_ is an experimental surrealist comic. It is a meditation on memory, and was created in a square format to both accommodate and subvert the fickleness of social media.

The comic is written, illustrated, and fully colored by me. Click the buttons above to preview the first four pages!
